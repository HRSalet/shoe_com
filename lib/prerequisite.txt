1. Install Xampp server into your system.

2. Goto C: Drive -> xampp -> htdocs -> paste "shoe_hive_db" folder in this location.

3. Make sure both your laptop/pc and your mobile device should be connect to the same wifi network.

4. Open cmd and type ipconfig and press enter to see the IP address. Replace your IP address into the shoe_model.dart file in line no. 26 and into the view\home\body.dart line no. 47.
